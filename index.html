Hush in the Hollow

By: Rachel Diamond

About the Game

Hush in the Hollow is a stealth-based board game set in a magical forest that has been overtaken by a goblin town at its center. Players begin on the outskirts of the forest and must navigate inward, uncovering a hidden and ever-changing map as they move.

Along the way, players will collect treasures and encounter goblins—some of which can be fought or avoided depending on strategy. However, the real danger lies in the goblin army at the center of the forest. Every action risks alerting the growing forces within the goblin town, increasing tension as players move closer to their goal.

The game blends exploration, combat, and stealth, creating a balance between progression and caution in a constantly shifting environment revealed piece by piece.

How to Play

Players move across a hidden tile-based board revealed as they explore. Each tile may contain treasures, goblins, or hazards. Some goblins can be fought, while others are best avoided. The goal is to navigate toward the goblin town at the center without alerting too many enemies or triggering overwhelming danger.

Game Elements

The Board

The board is a modular, hidden system revealed as players move through the forest.

The Characters

Players choose characters with different abilities that affect movement and survival.

The Cards

Cards introduce events, abilities, and challenges throughout gameplay.

How It Was Made

Fabrication

The physical components were laser cut, 3D printed, and assembled to create a modular board system.

Electronics

Neopixels, hall effect sensors, and servos were used to detect movement and trigger game events.

Design Tools

Adobe Illustrator and Procreate were used for card and visual design development.

Development Process

The game evolved through multiple iterations, playtests, and feedback sessions that refined both mechanics and physical design.

Final Results

The final build combines physical interactivity, hidden information, and dynamic exploration to create an immersive board game experience.
