How to Play

Objective

Be the first player to reach 50 Mana Points.

Setup

  • Each player chooses a Character Card.
  • All players begin at the outer edge of the board on one of 5 white tiles.
  • Shuffle all decks, keeping each color separate.

Turn Structure

  1. Roll the dice.
  2. Move until you light up a tile.
  3. Pick up a card.

Tile Encounters

Enemy Tiles — Green

  • Draw an Enemy Card and roll.
  • Success: Gain XP.
  • Failure: Weakened next turn.
  • Damage: Roll again.

Treasure Tiles — Blue

  • Draw a Treasure Card.
  • Success: Gain treasure.
  • Failure: Goes to bottom of deck.
  • Destroyed: Lost.

Resource Tiles — Yellow

  • Draw a Resource Card:
    • Gold Coins
    • Potions
    • Armor / Weapons
    • Sellable Resources

Goblin Army Tile — Red

When the goblin town is alerted, a red card is drawn. Players across the board who did not draw the card may choose to join the battle or decline.

  • Success: Mana Points are divided between all participating players.
  • Failure: All participating players are injured for the next round.

Player Combat

  • If players land on the same tile, a duel is initiated.
  • Players bet Mana Points.
  • The winner collects the Mana Points.
